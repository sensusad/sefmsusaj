About Us

Established in 1998 AD, Radio Adhyatma Jyoti 104.8 MHz is a spiritual radio station based in Kathmandu, Nepal. Operated by FM Adhyatma Jyoti Pvt Ltd, The station is dedicated to providing high-quality programming that promotes spirituality and infotainment. It offers 18 hours of daily programming, seven days a week, covering a variety of topics, including meditation, yoga, self-improvement, and spirituality. The station features live shows, as well as pre-recorded content, including talks by spiritual leaders, interviews with individuals who have had spiritual experiences, and devotional music.

Radio Adhyatma Jyoti 104.8 MHz provides a platform for spiritual organizations and leaders to share their message with a wider audience. Its aim is to promote the values of peace, harmony, and spirituality among its listeners and inspire them to learn about spiritual practices and connect with their inner selves. As a leading spiritual radio station in Nepal, Radio Adhyatma Jyoti 104.8 MHz is dedicated to delivering exceptional service and measurable results.

Mission

Our mission at Radio Adhyatma Jyoti 104.8 MHz is to promote spirituality and infotainment through high-quality programming that inspires and uplifts our listeners. We strive to create a platform for spiritual leaders and organizations to share their message and connect with a wider audience, promoting values of peace, harmony, and personal growth.

Vision

Our vision at Radio Adhyatma Jyoti 104.8 MHz is to remain the leading spiritual radio station in Nepal, inspiring listeners to explore spirituality and connect with their inner selves. We aim to be a trusted resource for high-quality programming that promotes values of peace, harmony, and personal growth, contributing to the well-being of our listeners and the wider community.
